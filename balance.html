Balance

Next-gen budgeting

Balance is a mobile budgeting app designed to help next-generation budgeting more convenient and stress-free. We want to create a mobile platform for users to easily (1) view and customize budget summary, (2) retain “mindset of budgeting” in a digital space, (3) and manage their transactions and budget plans.

1. Homescreen

We created a home screen to showcase user’s budgeting and financial summary in a visual way through graphs and charts. These graphs and charts are like widgets displayed on a vertical dashboard. Users can click on the three circles (meatball menu) on the top right to reorder them or remove them. Users can also tap the “+” circle to select/deselect certain graphs and charts.

2. Drag & Drop

Users can navigate to the Drag & Drop screen to categorize their transactions. They can drag their transactions displayed at the bottom towards a desired category circle. The most recently sorted transaction would show up under the category name with a back arrow for users to easily undo. When a category circle is pressed, users will be directed to a list of transactions sorted under that category. They can also tap the list icon on the top right to view all sorted transactions.

3. Log

Users can navigate to the Log screen to edit existing transactions or add an offline transaction. Users can also create a new budget plan or reuse a previous budget plan. Users will be able to refer to their previous month’s performance to readjust their budget plan.
